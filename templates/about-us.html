All About SandE

What is SandE?

SandE is an autonomous sand filtering robot that is capable of filtering small to medium sized pollutants from dry sand.

What are the motivations behind building SandE?

- Growing concern over environmental pollution, particularly in sandy areas where traditional cleanings may be impractical​     - SandE proposes a relatively affordable cleaning alternative - Autonomous cleaning alternative

When was SandE built?

The idea was first proposed in September 2023. The team started putting the concept together from September to December 2023. The robot was assembled between February to April 2024.

What's the future work for SandE look like?

SandE project was backed up by ECE 4872, an ECE senior design class at Georgia Institute of Technology. The budget given for the project was $750. With this, the components chosen were limited. Given in the future, more budget's allocated to SandE, we can select a better motor, higher battery capacity, and more efficient trash collecting system.
